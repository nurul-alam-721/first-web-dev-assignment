Innovation

Innovation elevates productivity by introducing novel concepts, methods, and technological advancements.

Sustainability

Infrastracture

Empowerment

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastracture

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"A world without poverty, unemployment, and inequality. This is my vision, my mission, my dream. To build a society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive, where dignity and justice prevail, and where human potential is unleashed. I believe in a world where access to quality education, healthcare, and basic necessities are fundamental human rights. A world where economic growth benefits all, not just a select few. A world where compassion, empathy, and solidarity guide our actions. This is the world I strive to create, a world where every individual can live a life of meaning and purpose."

Dr. Mohammad Yunus

Chief Advisor of the people republic of Bangladesh
